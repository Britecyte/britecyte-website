What Lipoderma is

Restoration, not augmentation.

Like-for-like human adipose allograft

Aging, weight loss, and time thin the healthy adipose tissue that gives the face and hands their soft structure. Lipoderma restores what was lost with native adipose—fat for fat—rather than filling space with a synthetic gel.

Allogeneic & ready to use

Built on Britecyte's immunogenicity-resolved platform, Lipoderma is an off-the-shelf allograft free from synthetic agents. It is available in 1.5 cc vials for precision placement and 10 cc vials for larger-volume restoration in the face, hands, and areas affected by GLP-1-associated volume loss.

Select physician network

Lipoderma is available exclusively through a selective network of physicians who love the product—fewer providers, deeper expertise, and outcomes consistent with the tissue's native behavior.

Restoration areas

Midface and under-eye hollows, temples, dorsal hands, and volume loss following rapid weight reduction—including GLP-1-assisted weight loss. Each restoration plan is determined by a qualified provider during consultation.
